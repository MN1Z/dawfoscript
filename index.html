Join a friend on Bedrock

  1. Open Minecraft Bedrock/Pocket Edition.
  2. Go to Play → Friends.
  3. Ensure both players have each other added as Xbox friends.
  4. Join the world when your friend is online with multiplayer enabled.

Join a Realm

  1. In Minecraft, go to Play.
  2. Use Join Realm and enter a valid invite code.
  3. Accept invite permissions from the Realm owner.
